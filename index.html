Ember London is a community for anyone interested in Ember.js and building ambitious applications in the browser.

We host monthly meetups with talks from contributors, library authors and enthusiasts. The aim is to cover a broad range of topics of interest to beginners and advanced users alike.

Subscribe to our mailing list for updates on events.
