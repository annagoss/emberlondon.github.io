Ember London

Ember London is a community for anyone interested in Ember.js and building ambitious applications in the browser.

We host monthly meetups with talks from contributors, library authors and enthusiasts. The aim is to cover a broad range of topics of interest to beginners and advanced users alike.

Subscribe to our mailing list for updates on events.

When is Ember London?

The next Ember London is slated for Wednesday 15th January, 2014. 6.30pm – 9.30pm.

Details to be confirmed in the new year.

What to Expect

In-depth talks and discussion with other developers building ambitious web applications. Our focus is Ember.js, but we strive to cover other great technologies such as Angular, Knockout and more.

After the talks we’ll head to pub to continue the conversation.
